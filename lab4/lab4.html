Challenge

Create pseudocode for a javascript based game; in this case, a game of Rock, Paper, Scissors.

Problems

Slight complications arose when trying to figure out what information in our high-level lists would be appropriate or not. That being said, by working as a group we both managed to extract and include only the most neccessary information.

Reflection

This specific lab made us reconsider organizational styles as well as well as reflect on what information is crucial and a must-have in our code and what can be discarded due to its repetetive and/or irrelevant nature. This assignment served as an important tool when it comes to changing how we perceieve and understand language in javascript.

Results

Changing a Flat Tire.

  1. Locate your spare tire and jack.
  2. Remove spare tire, jack, and lug wrench from their storage location.
  3. Use the lug wrench to remove bolts from tires.
  4. Ensure that bolts are loosened in the order that represents the shape of the star.
  5. Place the jack under the vehicle.
  6. Ensure that it is aligned to one of the four jack points under the vehicle.
  7. Turn the jack so that it slowly lifts the car.
  8. Make sure the car is lifted high enough so that the tire can be removed.
  9. Remove old tires.
  10. Place the old tire to the side.
  11. Place the spare tire where the flat tire was located.
  12. Ensure that the spare tire is aligned properly.
  13. Tighten bolts on the spare.
  14. Tighten them enough so that the tire does not fall off when you lower the car.
  15. Slowly lower the car with the jack.
  16. Once the car is back on the ground, use the lug wrench to ensure that bolts are tightened on the tire.
